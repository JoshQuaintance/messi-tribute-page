The Start Of The Legend

At the age of 4 Lionel Messi joined the local club Grandoli where his dad coached him. Eventually he switched to a different club and scored almost 500 goals for them. This is when people starts to talk about the new young talent.

When His Dreams Took A Hit

When he was 11 years old, he was diagnosed with Growth Hormone Deficiency. Player of The Year

In 2009, at the age of 22, Lionel Messi won the Ballon d'Or. He also won theFIFA Player Of The Year. Because of this, FIFA agreed to merge with Ballon d'Or and became FIFA Ballon d'Or.

Retirement?!?!?

In 2016, after missing a penalty as Argentina lost a shoot-out to Chile in the Copa America final, Lionel Messi retired. The 29-year old Barcelona quits the international soccer. This event made a big headline in Europe, people are confused.

Decisions Turned

In August of 2016, Messi announced that he is coming back out of retirement to help his national team win the world cup. He said, "I seriously thought about leaving, but my love for Argentina and this jersey forced me to return." Then he eventually came back to Barcelona again. Currently, Messi is in Barcelona and Argentina as #10 Forward.
